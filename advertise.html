Teleport directly to the 3 major cities to broadcast your venue's shout ads. Enable world hopping to automatically travel across your entire Data Center and reach maximum audiences.

Keep a Party Finder listing consistently active. The bot will maintain your PF both during your venue's open hours and during the crucial lead-up time before doors open.

Set up barker-style announcements to run automatically inside your house or venue. Broadcast in say, yell, or shout chat on a customized interval. The bot will automatically navigate back to its starting location after completing town ad runs.

Important Limitations

Compatibility Notes
Supported Regions Available for NA, EU, JP, and SA regions only. (Not compatible with China or Korea clients).
Housing Navigation If returning to a starting point inside a house, ensure there is a direct, clear path. The bot cannot see player-placed furniture and may get stuck trying to jump over desks or barriers.

Scheduling & Testing

⏱️ Phase Scheduling & Profiles

Customize your ad copy specifically for "Before Open" and "During" phases. Easily save, import, and export multiple profiles to switch between different venue nights on the fly.

Verify your timing and routing without spamming the community. Test Mode moves your character and creates Party Finders normally, but prints shout messages safely to your private /echo and RebornBuddy log instead of public chat.
