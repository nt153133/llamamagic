Submersible & Airship Expeditions

🚀 Automated Redeployment

Effortlessly resend active vessels on the same expedition they just completed. The botbase will also handle repairing ships to ensure there is no downtime.

📦 Smart Loot Storage

Automatically offload and store expedition rewards directly into your retainers or the Company Chest.

⚙️ Multi-Character Scaling

Settings are saved on a per-FC basis, meaning the exact same storage and routing settings can be seamlessly used across multiple characters in the same Free Company.

🔗 Automation Hooks

Includes built-in Lisbeth and Orderbot hooks for advanced users looking to integrate workflows.

Compatibility & Limitations

Important Information
Client Support Fully supports Global (NA/EU/JP/SA) and Chinese clients.
Note: Traditional Chinese clients are not currently supported.
Route Limitations The bot will only resend ships on the same expedition they just finished.
Housing Projects Multi-stage housing items (e.g., Full Estate Exteriors) are not fully supported. The bot can finish the current stage, but will not chain stages automatically.
Lisbeth Required This botbase utilizes Lisbeth to craft the parts and requires an active Lisbeth license.

Workshop Crafting Automation

🛠️ Start-to-Finish Crafting

Fully automates the crafting of airship and submersible parts in the FC workshop. Start new projects from scratch, finish already-started projects, or queue up multiple of the same item.

⚔️ Autonomous Seal Grinding

Missing GC shop materials? The botbase will automatically grind Siren Song Sea for Grand Company seals and purchase the required items.

📊 Real-Time Overlay

Features an in-game overlay to track the live progress of your Lisbeth crafting orders, as well as run statistics during GC seal dungeon grinds.
